Tube Audio Options+

A firefox extension

Audio only playback:
- Completely disables video rendering when enabled.
- Switch between video and audio-only seamlessly.
Limitations:
- Doesn't work with live streams.
- On desktop using the YT miniplayer may have unexpected results.
- Depending on your network connection on some rare cases it may take a couple of seconds to get the audio-only stream.

Please, allow this permissions:
"Access your data for sites in the *://youtube.com domain" is required to change the video/audio source of the player.
"Access your data for sites in the *://googlevideo.com domain" is required to capture the audio-only stream.

* After granting permissions please remember to refresh any tab that may have been already open on youtube.com for the extension to work correctly.

** Works best alongside an adblocker like ublock Origin.

Permissions Granted!

Desktop: Use the headphones toggle on the lower right corner of the video player to enable/disable the extension.


Mobile: Use the floating button on the lower left corner of the screen to enable/disable the extension.


DISABLED      ENABLED



*If the toggle (or button on mobile) aren't visible try refreshing the page and/or changing the selected video.
