Tube Audio Options+

A firefox extension

Audio only playback:
- Completely disables video rendering when the audio only toggle is enabled.
- Switch between video and audio seamlessly.
Limitations:
- Doesn't work on live streams.
- Using the miniplayer may have unexpected results.

Please, grant permissions manually:
"Access your data for sites in the *://youtube.com domain" is required to change the video/audio source of the player.
"Access your data for sites in the *://googlevideo.com domain" is required to capture the audio-only stream.
